Elena Rossi

Master Glassblower & Founder

"Glass is alive. It moves, it breathes, and it remembers. My role is not to force it, but to guide it."

From the historic furnaces of Murano to her modern studio in the mountains, Elena has dedicated her life to the art of glass. Her work explores the intersection of traditional technique and contemporary form, often finding inspiration in the natural world.

Our Journey

2005

Elena begins her apprenticeship in Venice, learning the secrets of the maestros.

2010

Aurora Glass Studio is founded in a small garage with a single furnace.

2015

First major exhibition "Liquid Light" tours across Europe.

2020

Expanded to a larger facility and launched the Masterclass Workshop program.

2026

Celebrating over two decades of artistic excellence with a new global collection.

Philosophy & Techniques

Sustainable Art

We believe in responsible creation. Our studio utilizes 100% renewable energy and recycles all glass cullet into new, beautiful forms.

The Human Touch

In a world of mass production, we celebrate the imperfect perfection of the human hand. No two pieces are ever identical.

Collaboration

We work closely with interior designers and architects to create site-specific installations that harmonize with their environment.
