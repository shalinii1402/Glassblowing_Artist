Our Commission Process

We work closely with clients to create bespoke glass art that perfectly fits their vision and space. Whether it's a statement chandelier, a set of custom stemware, or a sculptural centerpiece, the process is collaborative and personal.

1

Consultation

Discuss your ideas, budget, and space requirements to align on a shared vision.

2

Design & Sketching

We provide detailed sketches and select color palettes for your approval before work begins.

3

Hot Shop Creation

Elena expertly crafts your custom piece in the furnace, shaping molten glass with precision.

4

Delivery or Install

Your masterpiece is securely packaged for worldwide shipping or professionally installed.
