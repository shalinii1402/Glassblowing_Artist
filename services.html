Our Premium Services

Beyond creating art, we offer specialized services to integrate glass into your life and spaces. Explore how we can bring your vision to light.

Custom Lighting Design

Illuminate your space with bespoke glass lighting. From grand chandeliers to delicate pendant lights, we design and fabricate unique fixtures that serve as both light sources and breathtaking art pieces.

  • Bespoke Chandeliers & Installations
  • Hand-blown Pendants & Sconces
  • Lighting Design Consultation

Architectural Installations

We collaborate with architects and interior designers to create large-scale glass installations. Whether it's a corporate lobby, a hotel atrium, or a private residence, our glass art transforms environments into immersive experiences.

  • Site-Specific Artworks
  • Structural Glass Features
  • Large Scale Fabrication & Rigging

Restoration & Repair

Preserve the legacy of your glass treasures. Our expert restoration services breathe new life into antique stained glass, chipped sculptures, and damaged heirlooms, using traditional techniques to ensure authenticity and durability.

  • Antique Stained Glass Repair
  • Cold-Working & Polishing
  • Invisible Bonding Restoration

Workshops & Education

Step into the hot shop and experience the magic of glassblowing. We offer private and group workshops ranging from beginner encounters to advanced masterclasses, all taught by professional artists in a safe, inspiring environment.

  • Beginner Blow-Your-Own Classes
  • Advanced Technique Masterclasses
  • Corporate Team Building Events

Corporate Gifting & Awards

Elevate your recognition with hand-crafted glass awards. We design prestigious trophies and corporate gifts that reflect excellence and sophistication, making them the perfect way to honor achievements and milestones.

  • Custom Awards & Trophies
  • Executive Gifting
  • Logo & Brand Integration

Ready to Start Your Project?

Whether you need a custom installation, a restoration, or a unique workshop experience, we are here to bring your vision to glass. Let's create something extraordinary together.
