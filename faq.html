Frequently Asked Questions

Do you ship internationally?

Yes, we ship safely to most countries. Shipping costs are calculated at checkout or during the commission process.

How long does a commission take?

Standard commissions take 4-8 weeks depending on complexity. Large installations may take longer.

Is the glass food safe?

Yes, all our tableware is made from lead-free crystal and is safe for food and drink.

What if my piece arrives broken?

We insure all shipments. In the rare event of breakage, please contact us within 24 hours with photos for a replacement to be arranged.
